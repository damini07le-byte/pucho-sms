About Pucho Public School

Pucho Public School is a well-established educational institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development. With experienced faculty, modern classrooms, and a student-focused approach, we aim to prepare learners for a bright future. Our school combines traditional values with contemporary teaching methodologies to create an environment where every student can thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Academics

Primary Education

Building strong foundations in core subjects with interactive learning methods and age-appropriate curriculum designed to spark curiosity and love for learning.

Secondary Education

Comprehensive curriculum focusing on conceptual understanding, critical thinking, and preparing students for board examinations with experienced faculty guidance.

Higher Secondary Education

Specialized streams in Science, Commerce, and Arts with advanced learning resources, practical labs, and career counseling for future success.

Co-curricular Activities

Sports, arts, music, dance, and cultural programs to ensure holistic development and help students discover their talents beyond academics.

Digital Learning Support

Smart classrooms, computer labs, and online learning platforms to enhance education with modern technology and prepare students for the digital age.

Smart School Management

Pucho Public School leverages modern digital systems to provide seamless communication and efficient management for students, parents, and staff.

Online Admissions Management

Streamlined digital admission process with online forms, document submission, and real-time application tracking.

Digital Fee Tracking

Transparent fee management system with online payment options, instant receipts, and detailed payment history.

Attendance Monitoring

Real-time attendance tracking accessible to parents and staff for better monitoring of student presence.

Exam & Result Management

Digital examination system with online result publication, performance analytics, and progress reports.

Parent Communication Portal

Dedicated portal for parents to stay connected with teachers, access updates, and monitor their child's progress.

Admissions Open

Admissions are currently open for the academic year 2025–26. Parents can contact the school office for admission procedures and details. We welcome students who are eager to learn and grow in a nurturing educational environment.
